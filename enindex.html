About us

Longrun Financial Group Co., Ltd. ("Longrun Financial Group") is a futures trading company regulated by the Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission (SFC), with the Central Entity Number(CE No.) as AYB226. The company is licensed by the SFC to deal with the Hong Kong and global futures and options contracts and risk management businesses permitted by laws and regulations.

The company has a strict internal control system and experienced professionals.We are familiar with the operation of the global futures market and client oriented. We are doing our best to provide investors with high-quality and fast global futures and options trading and information services,and provide enterprises and industries with a wide range of services of hedging and risk management. The company insists the business philosophy of "discovering value, creating value, sharing value", advocates a corporate culture of "integrity, innovation, pragmatism, efficiency, excellence, and inclusiveness", adhere to compliance management, stable development, and aims to create value for customers and to share global investment opportunities.


LONGRUN WEALTH MANAGEMENT LIMITED was established in 2017 with the registration number of the Hong Kong Federation of Insurance Consultants (CIB Reg. No.: 0544). We have always provided diversified professional services for insurance and financial planning to customers around the world and China region through good international and domestic connections. As an emerging insurance brokerage company in Hong Kong, our cooperation network will be further expanded, based in Hong Kong and close to the market, we are committed to finding suitable protection for our customers, and truly satisfying the needs of customers for personal protection, asset allocation, and wealth inheritance. We have the advantages of both international vision and local knowledge, and we are confident to provide customers with more comprehensive and in-depth services. Longrun will continue to improve its team and service quality, not only to progress with the market, but also to grow together with customers, and go through every stage of life hand in hand.
